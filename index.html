About Nexus Analyst

**Nexus Analyst** is designed as an advanced AI-driven research tool that provides preliminary investment analysis by integrating textual market context with visual technical chart analysis. Our goal is to augment the initial stages of financial due diligence.

Our Mission

We leverage multimodal generative models to create integrated assessments, helping users quickly identify technical patterns, fundamental drivers, and potential risks associated with a given stock or market sector.

CRITICAL: Investment Risk Disclaimer

**Nexus Analyst is NOT a financial advisor.** The analysis provided is purely algorithmic, based on historical and publicly available data, and is intended for **research, educational, and informational purposes only.**

Do not use this output as the sole basis for making investment decisions. All investment involves risk, including the loss of principal. Consult with a qualified and regulated financial professional before making any trades or investments.

Privacy Policy

This policy outlines the data practices for the Nexus Analyst tool. We prioritize user privacy, especially given the financial domain.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • **Personal Financial Information:** We never ask for or store your bank details, trading account information, or personal investment portfolio data.
  • **Personally Identifiable Information (PII):** We do not intentionally collect names, addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses.

Data Collection and Processing

  • **Queries and Images:** The text queries and uploaded images are processed by the generative AI API solely for the purpose of generating the analysis result. They are not stored long-term or used to identify you.
  • **Public Market Data:** Our analysis relies on accessing publicly available stock and market data via Google Search grounding. This data is non-personal.

By using this tool, you consent to the temporary processing of your input data for analytical output generation.
